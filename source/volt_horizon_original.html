Philosophy

Four pillars, one atmosphere.

Every design choice in Volt Horizon answers to these four principles. If a component does not serve all four, it does not ship.

P · 01
Pre-dawn, not midnight.
Dark, but never dead. Our base surface carries a hint of teal — the sky just before the sun shows up. Warm enough to sit with for 20 minutes.
P · 02
One volt, not many.
A single hot accent does all the heavy lifting. No purple gradients. No rainbow charts. Restraint is the luxury.
P · 03
Data as texture.
Numbers are not decoration. Monospace digits, glowing metric bars, live-assembling BOQ lines — the math is the hero.
P · 04
AI has a body.
Pria is never a faceless chat box. Her presence is a slow-rotating chromatic avatar and a warm gradient that follows her words.

Motion

Motion tells the truth.

Three signature motions carry the product. Every other animation is a variation of these. Keep it restrained — in Volt Horizon, the reward for waiting is one beautiful moment, not fifteen jittery ones.

M · 01 · PULSE
The heartbeat.
A soft voltage ring expanding out from any "live" element — Pria's avatar, the total-cost number, the active step marker. Slow (1.8s), rare, not on every hover.
M · 02 · ASSEMBLE
Numbers ticking in.
BOQ lines and metric values ease up with a staggered counter, cubic-out. Used whenever the plan recomputes — the feeling of work being done in real time.
M · 03 · HORIZON
Chromatic spin.
Pria's avatar slowly rotates a conic gradient of all three accent hues. Always on, always subtle (8s/rev). This is her pulse — and the only place we use the full palette at once.

Guardrails

How to keep it Volt Horizon.

These rules are the difference between distinctive and generic. If a designer or engineer is tempted to break one, push back.

DO
  • Anchor every screen to one hot voltage element. One, not two.
  • Use mono for every displayed number the user will act on.
  • Let dark surfaces breathe — minimum 24px padding on any card.
  • Reserve bio-green for "the system is producing / live" states.
  • Give Pria her chromatic avatar whenever she speaks.
  • Use gradient text sparingly and only on the single hero number.
  • Stagger reveals in order of importance, not left-to-right order.
DON'T
  • Use purple, cyan-gradient or neon-blue "AI" accents. Tired.
  • Put voltage and bio next to each other as CTAs — they fight.
  • Render financial data in Fraunces or Inter. Mono only.
  • Animate on every hover. Pulse is rare. Motion is earned.
  • Use pure black (#000) anywhere. Always route through void or surface-000.
  • Draw fake "tech" lines, nodes, circuits. The system is the tech.
  • Stack more than three colored glows in a single viewport.
