# Pricing AI

An AI-native platform that helps homeowners, SMEs, and businesses across Africa make confident, well-priced renewable energy decisions.

---

## The Problem

Anyone considering a renewable energy investment in Nigeria faces a slow, opaque, and vendor-biased journey. Getting a quote means contacting multiple installers, waiting days for responses, and receiving estimates in wildly different formats with no way to compare them fairly. Most people either accept the first reasonable-looking quote or give up and stay on diesel.

---

## What Pricing AI Does

A user describes what they want to power, where they are, and how they want to pay. Pria — the platform's AI agent — handles the rest: sizing the system, building a complete budget, and generating a ready-to-act plan in minutes.

Every completed plan includes:

- System design and bill of quantities (BOQ)
- 20-year cashflow model and payback analysis
- Project schedule (Gantt)
- PDF report and XLSX workbook
- Shareable plan link

---

## Features

- **Conversational intake** — Pria guides users through the process, adapting based on what she already knows
- **Load profile builder** — appliance selector, electricity bill upload (OCR), and Nigerian archetype presets
- **Sizing engine** — deterministic electrical sizing for solar PV, batteries, inverters, wind, and biomass
- **Pricing and BOQ** — component catalogue with economy, standard, and premium tiers; FX-linked pricing
- **Financial model** — cashflow, payback, NPV, IRR, and sensitivity analysis against fuel price and FX volatility
- **Generator comparison** — models the real Nigerian baseline: diesel/petrol generator cost, not a grid bill
- **Installer marketplace** — connects completed plans to vetted local installers for real quotes
- **Financing catalogue** — lists available loan and PAYG products with eligibility rules
- **Voice mode** — users can interact with Pria by voice without leaving the current screen

---

## Renewables Supported

Solar PV (off-grid, hybrid, on-grid) · Solar thermal · Small wind · Biomass / biogas · Micro-hydro · Hybrid combinations

---

## Vendor Neutral

Pricing AI does not sell panels, inverters, or installation. Revenue comes from installer lead fees and financing referrals — not hardware margins.
